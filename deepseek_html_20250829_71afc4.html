8-Step Change Model

A comprehensive guide to organizational transformation

1
Create Urgency
A clear, powerful narrative that captures the need for change.
2
Build Coalition
A passionate, diverse group to carry the change through.
3
Develop Vision
The ability to manage feelings to achieve goals.
4
Communicate Vision
Define and clearly communicate the change.
5
Empower Others
Enable the business to define, plan and carry out the change.
6
Short-Term Wins
Focus on enabling and promoting wins across the organization.
7
Consolidate Gains
Keep momentum going and consolidate gains across the organization.
8
Anchor Change
Integrate change into culture, systems and processes to make it stick.
