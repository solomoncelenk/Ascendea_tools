Journey Explanation

1. Unaware State

The audience is confused or frustrated but doesn't yet see your solution as the answer.

2. Hooked via Pain

Content mirrors their identity or pain points to create immediate relevance.

3. Awareness

Introduces the possibility that there's a better way to solve their problem.

4. Authority

Demonstrates credibility through proof points and success stories.

5. Objections

Addresses potential concerns or reasons they might resist moving forward.

6. Belief Reframe

Shifts their perspective to align with the solution's worldview.

7. Commitment

Clear call-to-action that moves them toward conversion.

8. Solution

Final invitation into your system or product offering.
