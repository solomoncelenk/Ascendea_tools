Real Scenario

The Medical Specialist's Search

01 / The Profile
A senior medical professional looking for long-term safety. They were considering a newer build in a high-growth area.
02 / What we found
Our audit showed that while the area was popular, 800 new homes were planned for the same street. This would have killed the growth for a decade.
03 / The Rejection
We ruled out the entire market. It looked good on the surface, but the facts showed it was a trap.
04 / The Decision
We redirected to an established house in a street where no new homes could be built. The capital was protected and the owner-occupier demand was high.
